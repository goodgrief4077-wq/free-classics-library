## Philosophy Classics

### Ancient Philosophy

#### Aristotle — The Metaphysics
- **Formats:** PDF, EPUB, Full Text
- **Download:** [Direct PDF](https://ia801309.us.archive.org/5/items/metaphysicsaris00arisgoog/metaphysicsaris00arisgoog.pdf)
- **Source:** Internet Archive / Bohn's Classical Library
- **License:** Public Domain
- **Significance:** Foundational text on ontology, causation, and the nature of reality. Contains Aristotle's analysis of substance, form, matter, and the Unmoved Mover.

#### Aristotle — Nicomachean Ethics
- **Formats:** EPUB, TXT, ZIP
- **Download:** [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/ethics08438gut)
- **Source:** Internet Archive
- **License:** Public Domain
- **Significance:** Systematic treatment of virtue ethics, happiness (eudaimonia), and the good life. The foundation of virtue ethics tradition.

### Download Tips

1. **EPUB vs PDF:** EPUB is better for e-readers and reflows text. PDF preserves original formatting.
2. **No Images EPUB:** Smaller file size (~70% smaller) if images not needed.
3. **Plain Text:** Best for feeding into AI tools for analysis.
4. **Batch Download:** Use wget or curl for command-line downloading.

## Recommended Reading Paths

### Path 1: Philosophy Foundations
1. Aristotle — Nicomachean Ethics (virtue ethics)
2. David Hume — Treatise of Human Nature (empiricism)
3. Immanuel Kant — Prolegomena (critical philosophy)
4. Georg Hegel — Philosophy of Mind (dialectics)

### Path 2: Psychology Foundations
1. William James — Principles of Psychology (Vol 1)
2. Hermann Ebbinghaus — Psychology (memory and learning)
3. Carl Jung — Theory of Psychoanalysis (analytical psychology)

### Path 3: AI & Technology
1. Demystifying Artificial Intelligence (overview)
2. Thinking with AI (humanities perspective)
3. Philosophy of Astrophysics (philosophy of science)
4. Augustus De Morgan, Polymath (history of mathematics/AI)

### Path 4: History & Civilization
1. Thucydides — Peloponnesian War (classical antiquity)
2. Edward Gibbon — Decline and Fall of Roman Empire (classical to medieval)
3. Norman Conquest of England (medieval)
4. Beard — History of United States (modern)
